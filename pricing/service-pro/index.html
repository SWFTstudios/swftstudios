Who it's for

Is Service Pro right for you?

Service Pro is built for owners who need a professional web presence and occasional updates — not a full marketing automation stack.

Ideal for

  • Local service providers (consultants, coaches, trades, studios)
  • Nonprofits and ministries with clear donation or contact goals
  • App or product launches that need a focused landing page
  • Businesses that already handle follow-up manually or via email

Not the best fit if

  • You need missed-call text-back, CRM pipelines, or review automation
  • You want a Shopify store with product catalog management
  • You need weekly content shoots included in the same retainer
