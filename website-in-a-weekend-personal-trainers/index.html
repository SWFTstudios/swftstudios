FAQ

Common questions

What do I need before we start?

Your Instagram handle, a clear idea of your services and pricing, any photos or videos you already have, and 30 minutes for a discovery call. We handle the rest.

Can you use my Instagram content?

Yes. We pull from your existing reels, posts, client results, and brand assets to build a site that feels like you — without starting from scratch.

What if I do not have professional photos?

No problem. We can work with phone content and Instagram assets for launch, and SWFT can film classes, workouts, and testimonials locally in Jersey City or NYC if you want upgraded visuals.

Can you film content for me?

Yes. SWFT offers optional content filming — classes, workouts, testimonials, and behind-the-scenes footage — so your launch looks premium from day one.

How fast can we launch?

Most Website in a Weekend builds go live within one weekend after we collect your content and clarify your offer on the discovery call.

Do I need Webflow, Squarespace, or another platform?

No. SWFT handles platform setup, hosting guidance, and launch. You get a site that works — without learning another tool.

Can you help with ads after the site launches?

Yes. We offer optional Meta ad creative and campaign support so your new landing page can start driving leads right after launch.
