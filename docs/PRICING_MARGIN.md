## Target margin snapshot (3 GHL clients on Starter)

Assumes ~$40–60/mo overhead alloc per website client and higher alloc for content (shoot time, tools, delivery).

| Content Starter | $600/mo | $0 | ~$80 | $0 | **~$520** |
| Content Growth | $1,200/mo | $0 | ~$120 | ~$150 | **~$930** |

### Bundle build margin ($1,200 setup)

Treat as a **discounted Service Pro build** (full Service Pro one-time is $1,500).

- Still profitable if scope stays tight: **up to 5 pages**, Webflow in client account, **2 revisions / 30 days**
- Do not add GHL, custom logo, or extra shoot days without upgrading tier or quoting add-ons
- Monthly $600 is Content Starter — half-day shoot, Canva-led assets, no freelancer logo work

---

## Profitability rules

1. **Never put GHL on Service Pro at $200/mo** — margin does not support dedicated sub-account + SMS.
2. **Upgrade to GHL Unlimited before client 4** on Growth/E-Commerce.
3. **SMS / phone usage:** rebill at cost or cap in contract; do not absorb heavy Twilio usage.
4. **Freelancer work:** reserve only on Content Growth+; logo/identity = quote or Content Pro.
5. **Content Starter scope:** half-day shoot, 10–15 photos, Canva templates — no custom logo or freelance design without upgrade.
6. **One-time builds:** 30-day window + 2 revisions; additional work = hourly or tier upgrade.
7. **Bundle upsell:** Growth (+$200/mo on website) is the path to GHL for bundle clients who need automations.

---

## When to re-run this model

- Adding a 4th GHL client (plan switch)
- Changing public tier prices
- Adding new tools with fixed monthly cost
- Hiring a part-time producer or freelancer on retainer
- Material change in shoot/edit time per content tier
