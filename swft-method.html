Build your website plan

7 focused questions — about 5 minutes. The more you share, the better your site will perform. At the end you'll see exactly what we'll build and what it costs.

STEP 1 / ABOUT YOU
1 / 7

Let's start with you.

Before we talk websites, we want to understand the life you're building — not just the business.

Where are you working from? How many hours? What's changed vs. today?
Be honest — this shapes everything we build.

What do you do — and why you?

The best websites don't just list what you sell. They make it obvious why you're the only choice.

Be specific — products, services, packages, experiences. Name them.
What changes for your customer after they buy from you — emotionally, practically, visually?
What do you do that no one else does exactly like you? Your background, method, personality, results?

Tell us about who you serve.

A site that converts speaks directly to one person. Let's get inside their head.

Not just age and location — what do they want, fear, spend money on, talk about? What keeps them up at night?
What's the gap between what's out there and what you offer?
A real quote, a DM, a review — copy-paste it. This is gold for your website copy.

What do you want this site to do for you?

Every page, every button, every word we write is designed around your #1 goal.

Numbers are helpful — orders per week, monthly revenue, bookings filled, etc.
No judgment — time, money, not knowing where to start, imposter syndrome?

What should your brand feel like?

Your site should feel like you — instantly. The right look and feel turns a visitor into a buyer before they read a single word.

Think about how you'd describe your Instagram feed to someone who hasn't seen it.
Drop 1–3 links or handles — even if they're in a totally different industry. We just want the energy.

What does your site need to do?

Check everything you want — your price updates live based on what you choose. Don't hold back; we'll make it work.

Pro tip: Focus on what serves your customer's journey, not just what looks impressive. The best sites do one thing exceptionally well.
